Financial Support for
Island Farmers.

Access up to $1,000,000 in low-cost operating capital through the Advance Payments Program.

Interest-Free

The first $250,000 of your advance is interest-free, funded by the federal government.

Interest-Advantaged

Amounts above $250,000 are provided at Prime - 0.75%, negotiated specifically for PEI producers.

Local Guidance

PEIFA handles the paperwork, inspections, and liaison with financial institutions for you.

Advances are established based on anticipated yields and storage capacity.

2 Livestock Advance Streams

Standard Flow (Beef Cattle)

Up to 24-month repayment schedule. Best for long-term breeding and marketing cycles.

  • • Repayments made on an ongoing basis
  • • 100% ownership required

Continuous Flow (Hogs & Lambs)

Up to 16-month repayment schedule. Designed for high-turnover inventory operations.

  • • Sales invoices required upon repayment
  • • Repayments due at end of advance year

Requirements & Costs

Application Checklist

  • BRM Security

    Advance must be secured by AgriInsurance or AgriStability.

  • Business Status

    Open to individuals, partnerships, or corporations.

  • Citizenship

    Must be a Canadian citizen or Permanent Resident.

  • Ownership

    Producer must have 100% ownership of the commodity.

Trilateral agreement includes AAFC, PEIFA, and RBC Royal Bank.
