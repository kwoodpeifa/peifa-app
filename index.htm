Financial Support for
Island Farmers.

Access up to $1,000,000 in low-cost operating capital through the Advance Payments Program.

Interest-Free

The first $250,000 of your advance is interest-free, funded by the federal government.

Interest-Advantaged

Amounts above $250,000 are provided at Prime - 0.75%, negotiated for PEI producers.

Local Guidance

PEIFA handles the paperwork, inspections, and liaison with creditors for you.

Designed for PEI Production Cycles

Whether you are waiting for crops to grow, livestock to reach market weight, or stored product to be sold, the APP provides the cash flow needed to keep your operation moving.

$1M Max Advance
0% Interest (up to $250k)
!

Important Program Date

The application window for the 2026-2027 program year opens on April 1st. Early applications ensure faster funding once product is in the ground or inventory is verified.
